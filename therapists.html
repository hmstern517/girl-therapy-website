Why Girl Therapy

A platform that actually understands your clients — and you.

You already know that women's mental health needs aren't one-size-fits-all. Relationship patterns, body image, ambition and guilt, family dynamics, identity, the whole "why am I like this" spiral — it's specific work, and it deserves a platform built around it.

Girl Therapy's clinical identity is built on three pillars: somatic, attachment-based, and IFS (Internal Family Systems) therapy — with EMDR available as a specialized offering. If that's how you already practice (or how you've been wanting to), you're exactly who we're building this for.

give therapy 💛

Your work expands access

Every client who books with us helps fund a session for a woman who couldn't otherwise access care. As a Girl Therapy therapist, your work directly expands access to mental health care for women who need it most.

Founding therapists will have a real voice in shaping how Girl Therapy works — and early access as we build toward launch.
