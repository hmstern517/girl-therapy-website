The real talk

You've been doing this alone for too long.

You're told you can have it all — the career, the relationships, the body, the glow-up. What nobody tells you is who's supposed to hold it all together while you're busy becoming her.

So you overthink. You future-trip. You compare your inside to everyone's outside. And somewhere along the way, "I should probably talk to someone" became a thought you've had a hundred times and acted on zero.

That's not a personal failing. That's a system that wasn't built for you.

"Hot girls go to therapy" — and we mean that with our whole chest.

Our approach

Therapy that actually gets how your brain and body work.

Every therapist on Girl Therapy is trained in an approach built around three things we know matter most for women's mental health.

somatic

Your body keeps score too.

Anxiety isn't just in your head — it's in your shoulders, your stomach, your sleep. Somatic therapy helps you understand what your body's been trying to tell you, so healing isn't just talked about. It's felt.

attachment

The patterns started somewhere.

That thing where you either disappear in relationships or white-knuckle control? There's a reason. Attachment-based therapy helps you understand your patterns — with partners, friends, family, even yourself — so you can finally write a different story.

IFS

All your "parts" are welcome here.

The overachiever. The people-pleaser. The one who's "fine." IFS therapy helps you get to know every part of yourself — even the messy ones — without judgment. Healing isn't about silencing parts of you. It's about understanding them.

Need something more specialized? We also offer EMDR with select therapists for deeper trauma work.

What makes us, us

Built different, on purpose.

All-female therapist network

Every therapist on our platform is a woman who specializes in the things women actually deal with — relationship patterns, imposter syndrome, body image, ambition and guilt, family dynamics, and more. No explaining yourself from scratch. No "well, have you tried yoga?"

Give Therapy

For every client who books with us, we help fund therapy for a woman who couldn't otherwise afford it. Access to mental health care shouldn't be a privilege. Give Therapy is how we make sure it isn't just one.

On your time, on your terms

Telehealth means therapy fits into your actual life — not the other way around. No waiting rooms. No driving across town. Just real support, wherever you are.
